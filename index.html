Our Mission

To create transformative and sustainable spaces that inspire, connect, and elevate communities. We are dedicated to innovative design solutions that blend functionality, aesthetics, and environmental responsibility, delivering excellence in every project through a collaborative and client-centered approach.

Our Vision

To be a leading architectural firm known for redefining the built environment in Bataan and beyond. We aspire to set new standards in design innovation, sustainability, and social impact, shaping a future where architecture harmonizes with nature and enhances the quality of life for all.

About Us

S+S Architectural Designs, founded by Sheena Sysing and Silver Herras, began with a shared vision of creating meaningful spaces. The firm operates as a partnership, with Sheena Sysing and Silver Herras holding ownership. There are plans to bring in strategic collaborators or investors to expand the firm's reach. Located in Bataan, the office is designed to foster creativity and collaboration and is equipped with state-of-the-art design tools and modern meeting spaces.

Our Services

Residential Design

S+S specializes in creating bespoke homes that reflect the unique personalities and lifestyles of their clients. Services include personalized design consultations to capture the essence of each client’s vision.

  • Custom home designs
  • Renovations and extensions
  • Integration of sustainable practices

Commercial Design

S+S focuses on designing environments that optimize functionality, branding, and user experience for commercial spaces. Services include:

  • Retail spaces
  • Hospitality design
  • Mixed-use developments
  • Industrial buildings

Urban Planning

S+S’s urban planning services focus on sustainable and efficient city planning, promoting quality living and environmental stewardship. This includes:

  • Master planning
  • Zoning analysis and land use strategy
  • Transportation and infrastructure integration
  • Public space design

Interior Design

The firm provides a full range of interior design services, balancing aesthetics and functionality. This includes:

  • Interior layout planning
  • Selection of materials, finishes, and furniture
  • Lighting and acoustics design
  • Custom interior elements
  • Color and texture coordination

Sustainable Architecture

S+S integrates sustainability in all its projects, aiming for eco-friendly solutions that reduce environmental impact. Their services include:

  • Passive design strategies
  • Energy-efficient building systems
  • Use of sustainable materials
  • Water conservation systems
  • LEED and other green building certifications

Project Management

S+S ensures that projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standard. Their project management services include:

  • Budgeting and cost control
  • Scheduling and timeline management
  • Coordination between contractors, engineers, and designers
  • Construction oversight
  • Quality assurance and post-construction services
